ENG:
We are planning and designing a strategy game that will model the worlds and aliens war. In the game, the objects of the earth are divided into two as soldiers and farmers and alien objects are divided into two as cyborg and miner. The power of the worlders is calculated by multiplying the soldiers' weapons and number of defenses, and by the number of crops of farmers. The power of the aliens is calculated by multiplying the laser power and the number of defenses of the cyborg and by the number of miners. The program randomly selects three alien and worldly and randomly assigns the verifiable values of the objects belonging to these species between 0-10. When the power of objects of alien species is removed from the power of earthly objects, the game goal is achieved. The game must continue until the user enters -1 from the keyboard and the game point of that round must be printed on the screen for each round.
